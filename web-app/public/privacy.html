1. Information We DON'T Collect

Session Messenger is built on a zero-knowledge architecture:

  • No Personal Information: No names, phone numbers, email addresses, or contact lists
  • No Message Content: All messages are encrypted end-to-end and never stored on our servers
  • No User Profiles: No profile pictures, status messages, or personal details
  • No Location Data: No GPS tracking or location sharing
  • No Device Information: No device IDs, advertising IDs, or hardware fingerprinting
  • No Usage Analytics: No tracking of app usage, features used, or behavioral data
  • No Metadata: No message timestamps, recipient information, or communication patterns

2. How Session Messenger Works

MILITARY-GRADE ENCRYPTION

Your privacy is protected through advanced cryptographic techniques:

  • End-to-End Encryption: Messages encrypted with TweetNaCl cryptography before leaving your device
  • Perfect Forward Secrecy: Unique encryption keys for each message that are automatically deleted
  • Anonymous Accounts: Accounts created with cryptographically secure random keys
  • Local Storage Only: All data stored locally on your device in encrypted form
  • Disappearing Messages: Messages automatically delete after specified time periods

3. Data Storage

On Your Device:

  • Encrypted account keys (stored in device secure storage)
  • Message history (encrypted locally, deleted based on your settings)
  • App preferences (theme, notification settings)

On Our Servers:

  • Encrypted public keys for message delivery (no personal information attached)
  • Temporary encrypted message routing (deleted immediately after delivery)
  • NO message content, NO personal data, NO user information

4. Third-Party Services

Session Messenger is designed to minimize third-party dependencies:

  • Hosting: Vercel (infrastructure only, no data access)
  • No Analytics: No Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or tracking services
  • No Advertising: No ad networks or marketing platforms
  • No Cloud Backup: No iCloud, Google Drive, or cloud storage integration

5. Security Measures

ZERO-TRUST ARCHITECTURE
  • Client-Side Encryption: All encryption happens on your device
  • No Server-Side Decryption: We cannot decrypt your messages even if we wanted to
  • Secure Key Derivation: PBKDF2 with high iteration counts
  • Memory Protection: Sensitive data cleared from memory after use
  • Screenshot Protection: Prevents screenshots in sensitive areas (mobile)
  • Biometric Security: Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection

6. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights don't apply, but you have:

  • Complete Control: All your data is on your device under your control
  • Easy Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • No Account Recovery: We cannot recover lost accounts (by design for privacy)
  • Transparency: Open-source cryptographic implementations

7. Legal Compliance

  • GDPR Compliant: No personal data collection means no GDPR obligations
  • CCPA Compliant: No personal information sale or sharing
  • COPPA Compliant: No data collection from children
  • SOX Compliant: Enterprise-grade security standards

8. Law Enforcement

We Cannot Comply with Data Requests:

  • We don't store message content or user data
  • We cannot decrypt user communications
  • We don't have access to user identities
  • We cannot provide what we don't have
